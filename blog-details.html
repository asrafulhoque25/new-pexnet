1,400% Traffic Growth in 3 Months for SmileCrew Orthodontics

Pledged to serve the community, SmileCrew Orthodontics thrives on healthy smiles. Led by Dr. Connor Steiger, the firm has been providing quality dental health and preventive care to Leicester and the surrounding areas.

With PexNet's exceptional dental SEO services, SmileCrew had grown from 100 to 1,500 monthly visitors in just 3 months!

Challenges for SmileCrew

Even with quality dental care, they had significant gaps in their online presence. They approached us to improve their online visibility.

After a careful inspection, our team found issues with the website traffic and content quality. For instance:

Low organic visibility in Google Search

The website had minimal visibility in industry-specific search results. One of the main reasons was the poor use of keywords on the existing content quality.

Weak Google Maps rankings

They had an NAP inconsistency on their Google Business Profile, resulting in very weak Map-Pack visibility.

Limited service-area targeting

The website lacked clear specifics for the target of Leicester 73, and surrounding areas. In addition, the website lacked local SEO structures.

Only ~100 monthly website visitors

Inconsistent user experience and substandard content quality resulted in a very minimal (~100) monthly number of website visitors.

No strong keyword presence for high-intent orthodontic searches

High-intent keywords, such as 'Orthodontist in Leicester' was significantly missing, resulting in poor brand visibility.

What SmileCrew Needed

The client needed expert support to:

  • Higher rankings in local searches
  • Bring long-blog visibility
  • More qualified website visitors
  • More appointment bookings

Our Dental SEO Strategy

After a successful audit, the PexNet team had one clear goal: to improve SmileCrew's website's local visibility. To that end, the team started a 3-month aggressive local SEO strategy, drawing on 9+ years of experience working with dental clinics.

Analytics and Tools Setup

For the audit, our team had to set up analytical tools to measure our progress. For that, we picked:

  • Google Search Console for Crawling, Sitemaps Setup and Verification
  • Google Analytics 4 for Traffic and Conversion Tracking
  • Domain configuration (newsletter store)
