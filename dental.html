Why SEO for Dental Practices is Different

Dental patients search for care in different ways depending on their needs. Some look for emergency aid, while others are interested in Invisalign or implants. Here, factors like the type of the treatment, your location, and credibility matter most.

Examples of High-Intent Dental Searches

To be specific, you’ll try ranking for keywords like:

  • Dentist near me

  • Emergency dentist in [your location]

  • Dental implants in [your location]

  • Invisalign dentist near me

  • Cosmetic dentist in [your location]

Since traditional SEO can’t cater to these high-intent searches, your local dental SEO approach should be totally different.
